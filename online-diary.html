How it works

How to Use the Online Diary

01

Create a New Entry

Click the ＋ New Entry button in the sidebar. A fresh page opens with today's date pre-filled. Give it a title or leave it blank — it's your diary, your rules.

02

Set Your Mood & Tags

Pick an emoji mood from the dropdown (Happy, Sad, Anxious, Excited…) and add keyword tags like "work", "travel" or "family" to organize your entries over time.

03

Write Freely

Use the writing area to express your thoughts. Format text with the toolbar, or click ✨ Prompt for a writing prompt if you're not sure where to start.

04

Save & Reflect

Entries auto-save as you type. Browse past entries in the sidebar, filter by mood, or search keywords to revisit memories and track your emotional journey.

05

Export Your Diary

Use ⬇ Export to download all your diary entries as an HTML file — perfect for backup, printing or sharing a chapter of your life.

06

Build a Streak

The streak counter at the bottom tracks how many consecutive days you've written. Build healthy journaling habits and watch your streak grow! 🔥

What is it?

Your Free Private Online Diary

An online diary is a personal digital space where you record your thoughts, experiences, feelings and reflections. Unlike a physical notebook, a digital diary lets you search years of entries in seconds, never runs out of pages, and is always with you wherever you have a browser.

WebNotePad's online diary app stores everything in your browser's local storage — meaning your entries never touch a server and remain 100% private. There is no account to create, no email to verify, no subscription to manage. Just open the page and write.

Research consistently shows that regular journaling reduces stress and anxiety, improves self-awareness, boosts memory, and helps process difficult emotions. The simple act of writing things down gives your thoughts structure and your feelings a safe outlet.
