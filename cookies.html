Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

At WebNotePad, we believe in transparency. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences. Your privacy matters to us.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences over time, making your browsing experience smoother and more efficient.

How we use cookies

WebNotePad uses cookies for strictly essential and functional purposes only. We do not use cookies for advertising, tracking across third-party sites, or any form of behavioral profiling.

Specifically, we use:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core features like saving your dark mode preference so it persists when you return.
  • Functional Cookies: These help us remember your previous actions (like your preferred notes view) to improve your experience.

All cookies used by WebNotePad are stored locally in your browser. We do not send any cookie data to our servers or any third parties.

🔒 Privacy by design

We only use cookies that make the site work better for you. No tracking. No profiling. No selling of your data.

Your cookie choices

You have full control over cookies. Most web browsers allow you to:

  • View the cookies stored on your device
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Receive a notification when a new cookie is set

Please note that if you choose to block all cookies, some features of WebNotePad (like saving your theme preference) may not work as intended. However, the core notepad functionality will still be available.

Third-party cookies

We do not use third-party cookies for analytics, advertising, or social media tracking. We believe your writing environment should be free from external surveillance.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. We encourage you to check this page periodically for any updates. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy or your privacy, please feel free to contact us.
