List Maker That Does It All

Every feature you'd expect from a premium list app, completely free.

All your lists are saved automatically to your browser's local storage — they're private, they're fast, and they're always there when you come back. No account required, no email verification, no subscription. Just open the page and start making lists.

Getting started

How to Use the List Maker

Creating and managing your lists takes seconds. Here's everything you need to know to get the most out of the tool.

01

Create a New List

Click the ＋ New List button or tap any quick-create button in the sidebar (Grocery, Shopping, To-Do, etc.). Choose your list type and give it a name. Your new list appears instantly and is ready to use.

02

Add Items

Type your item in the Add an item bar at the top of the editor and press Enter or click ＋ Add. Optionally set a priority level, due date, quantity, and note for each item before adding.

03

Manage & Organize

Click the checkbox to mark items complete. Drag items up and down to reorder them. Hover an item to reveal the edit (✎) and delete (✕) buttons. Use the sort menu to automatically sort by priority or due date.

04

Filter & Search

Use the Status filter to show all items, active only, or completed items. Use the Priority filter to focus on high-priority tasks. Search any keyword to instantly highlight matching items across your list.

05

Edit Item Details

Click the ✎ Edit button on any item to open the full edit panel. Update the item's text, priority, due date, quantity, and add a detailed note. All changes save automatically the moment you confirm.

06

Export & Share

Click ⬇ TXT or ⬇ HTML in the top bar to download your list as a file. Use ⎘ Copy to copy the entire list to your clipboard and paste it into an email, message, or document.
