What is it?

Free Online WordPad — Write Anything, Anywhere

WordPad Online is a free browser-based word processor that brings the familiar power of Microsoft WordPad directly to your web browser — with no installation, no account, and no subscription required. Open the page, click the document, and start writing immediately.

The classic Windows WordPad application has been a trusted companion for everyday writing since 1995. Our online WordPad alternative recreates that experience for the modern web, adding powerful features like a full ribbon toolbar, find and replace, table insertion, image embedding, custom fonts, paragraph styles, line spacing controls, zoom, dark mode, and multiple export formats.

Whether you're drafting a letter, writing an essay, creating a business document, or simply need a fast and clean text editor online — WordPad Online is the tool for the job. All your documents auto-save to your browser's local storage, and you can export them as HTML, plain text, or print directly to PDF at any time.

Getting started

How to Use WordPad Online

Everything you need to know to write, format, and export beautiful documents in minutes.

01

Open & Start Writing

The document editor opens instantly when you load the page. Click anywhere on the white page area and start typing. Your document title is shown in the title bar at the top — click to rename it or it will be used as the export filename.

02

Format with the Ribbon

Select any text in the document and use the Home tab ribbon to apply formatting. Choose a font family, set the font size in points, and apply bold, italic, underline, strikethrough, colors, or heading styles.

03

Structure with Headings

Use the Styles group in the Home ribbon to apply Heading 1, 2, 3, or 4 to give your document structure. Apply Blockquote for pull quotes. These styles make your document look professional and export cleanly to HTML.

04

Insert Tables & Media

Switch to the Insert tab to add tables, images, hyperlinks, horizontal rules, the current date and time, or page breaks. The table picker lets you choose grid dimensions by hovering over the cell grid.

05

Find & Replace Text

Press Ctrl+F or click the Find button in the Home ribbon to open the Find & Replace bar. Type your search term, navigate matches with Find Next/Prev, and use Replace or Replace All to make substitutions.

06

Export & Print

Go to the File tab in the ribbon. Click Export HTML to download a fully formatted web document, or Export TXT for plain text. Click Print to open your browser's print dialog and save as PDF.
