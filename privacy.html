Privacy Policy

At WebNotePad, we believe that your thoughts should remain yours. Our privacy philosophy is simple: we don't want your data, so we don't collect it.

1. Data Collection

WebNotePad does not require an account, email address, or any personal identification to use. We do not collect, store, or have access to the content of the notes you create. Your notes are stored entirely within your own browser's Local Storage.

2. Local Storage

Your notes are saved locally on your device. This means:

  • Notes never leave your computer or phone.
  • We cannot "see" or "recover" your notes if you clear your browser cache or lose your device.
  • Your data is not synced across different browsers or devices unless you manually export and import it.

3. Analytics & Cookies

We may use minimal, privacy-focused analytics to understand website traffic. These tools do not track individual users or collect "Personally Identifiable Information" (PII). We do not use advertising cookies.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other sites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by us, and we advise you to review their Privacy Policies.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
